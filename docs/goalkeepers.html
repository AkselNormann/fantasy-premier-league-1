Value

Most valuable goalkeepers are generally the ones who concede fewest goals, make most saves and keep most clean sheets.

Achievements

Goalkeepers achievements are metrics such as number of saves they ususally make, and the probability of them keeping a clean sheet, or saving a penalty.

Errors

Most notable goalkeeper errors metrics are their average goals conceded or probability of them getting a yellow cards.
